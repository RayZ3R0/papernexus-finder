• 
Calculate the difference between two scores by taking one from the other
 
•

Note: do not rank any differences of 0 and when adding the number of scores, do 
not count those with a difference of 0, and ignore the signs when calculating the 
difference
 
• 
Add up the ranks for positive differences 
 
• 
Add up the ranks for negative differences
 
• 
T is the figure that is the smallest when the ranks are totalled (may be positive or 
negative)
 
•
